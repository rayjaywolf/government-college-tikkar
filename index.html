ABOUT US

The Government Degree College Tikkar is co-educational institution and established by notification No. EDN-A_Kha (1).1/2015 Dated Shimla-02 Date 26 March 2016 of the Govt. of Himachal Pradesh. At Present the streams of Arts and Commerce are available to the students. It is presently working in the science block of the local senior secondary school Tikkar complex. It is being expected that The Government Degree College Tikkar, Shimla will have its own modern complex equipped with all modern facilities in the near future.
